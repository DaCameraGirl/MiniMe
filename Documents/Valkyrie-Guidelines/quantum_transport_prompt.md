# Quantum Transport Analysis Prompt

I'm trying to optimize quantum coherence in a topological insulator-based device operating at 4K. The structure has competing requirements: high carrier mobility needs minimal disorder, but topological protection requires some surface roughness to induce Berry curvature. How would you balance these factors while maintaining a bulk gap >200meV? Consider both material selection (e.g., Bi₂Se₃ vs. (Bi,Sb)₂Te₃) and heterostructure engineering approaches.
