# ✦ MiniMe — Angela's AI Playground

A collection of creative AI-powered web tools and experiments built by [@DaCameraGirl](https://github.com/DaCameraGirl).

## 🧚‍♀️ Twinkle Fairy — [TWINKLE_FAIRY.html](./TWINKLE_FAIRY.html)

An interactive glowing fairy that flies around your screen and knows a little bit about everything in the world.

- Click her to open a chat bubble
- Ask her anything — she searches Wikipedia live for real answers
- Click anywhere on the page to call her to that spot
- Leaves a sparkle trail as she flies
- Built with pure HTML/CSS/JS — no framework, no install, just open and go

---

## 🌍 International Jewelry Box Translator — [LIVE_TWINKLE.html](./LIVE_TWINKLE.html)

A live translator with mesmerizing twinkling word animations. Each translated word fades in like a star appearing in the night sky.

- Supports 27 languages
- Live translation after a short pause (no button needed)
- Words animate in one by one with glowing star effects
- Voice input, text-to-speech, favorites, and history
- Works offline for saved phrases
- Three themes: Midnight Neon, Aurora Circuit, Daylight Prism

---

## 🚀 AI Evaluator Pro — [app.js](./app.js)

A React app for evaluating AI responses using custom prompts and rubrics.

- Build prompts that reflect real-world tasks
- Design scoring rubrics
- Grade AI responses
- Export results as a report
